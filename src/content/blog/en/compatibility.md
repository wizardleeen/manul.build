### Supported Platforms

Manul is available as a native executable for major operating systems. Please verify your system meets the minimum requirements below.

| Platform | Architecture | Minimum Requirement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Linux** | **x64** | **Any Distribution** |
| **Linux** | **ARM64** | **Glibc 2.28+** <br> Requires Ubuntu 20.04+, Debian 10+, Fedora 29+, or RHEL/Oracle Linux 8+. |
| **macOS** | **Apple Silicon / Intel** | **macOS 11 (Big Sur)** or newer. |
| **Windows** | **x64 / ARM64** | **Windows 10 / Server 2022** or newer. |
